# INTcoin Development Roadmap

**2026-2031 Strategic Direction**

This document outlines the development focus areas for INTcoin over the next five years. Rather than specific milestones, this roadmap describes the technical directions and priorities that will guide development.

---

## Core Technology

### Post-Quantum Cryptography
Continuous advancement of quantum-resistant cryptographic implementations. Research and integration of new post-quantum algorithms as they mature through NIST standardization and academic review. Performance optimization of Dilithium signatures and Kyber key encapsulation.

### Consensus & Validation
Ongoing improvements to block validation efficiency. Research into consensus algorithm enhancements that maintain decentralization while improving throughput. Investigation of sharding approaches compatible with post-quantum security.

### Smart Contracts (IntSC)
Expansion of the IntSC virtual machine capabilities. Development of additional PQC-native opcodes. Gas optimization and execution efficiency improvements. Contract deployment and debugging tooling.

---

## Network Infrastructure

### Lightning Network
Full Lightning Network integration with post-quantum channel security. Multi-hop payment routing optimization. Watchtower services and channel backup solutions.

### Cross-Chain Interoperability
Atomic swap support with major cryptocurrencies. Bridge infrastructure for wrapped token transfers. Research into trustless cross-chain communication protocols.

### Peer-to-Peer Networking
Enhanced peer discovery and relay mechanisms. Tor and I2P network support. Geographic distribution optimization. DoS protection and network resilience improvements.

---

## User Experience

### Wallet Development
Desktop and mobile wallet improvements. Hardware wallet integration. Multi-signature support enhancements. Address book and transaction management features.

### Developer Tools
SDK development for multiple programming languages. Smart contract development frameworks. Testing and simulation environments. Documentation and educational resources.

### Integration APIs
REST and WebSocket API improvements. Merchant payment processing tools. Exchange integration support. Third-party application frameworks.

---

## Privacy & Security

### Transaction Privacy
Research into privacy-preserving transaction mechanisms compatible with post-quantum cryptography. Optional confidential transactions. Stealth address implementations.

### Network Privacy
Enhanced IP address protection. Dandelion++ transaction relay. Encrypted peer communications.

### Security Auditing
Continuous security review processes. Bug bounty programs. Third-party audit coordination. Formal verification of critical components.

---

## Ecosystem Growth

### Mining & Staking
RandomX mining optimization. Pool software development. Research into proof-of-stake hybrid models if community consensus develops.

### Governance
Development of on-chain governance mechanisms. Proposal and voting systems. Treasury management frameworks.

### Community Development
Developer grants and bounties. Educational content creation. Community tooling and utilities.

---

## Research Areas

### Cryptographic Advancements
Monitoring developments in post-quantum cryptography. Zero-knowledge proof integration research. Homomorphic encryption exploration for smart contracts.

### Scalability Solutions
Layer 2 technologies beyond Lightning. State channels and rollup research. Database and storage optimization.

### Protocol Evolution
Soft fork and hard fork planning processes. Backwards compatibility strategies. Network upgrade coordination mechanisms.

---

*This roadmap represents general development directions and priorities. Specific features and timelines are subject to community input, research outcomes, and development progress.*
