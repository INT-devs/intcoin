## About INTcoin

INTcoin is a quantum-resistant cryptocurrency built with:
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography**: Dilithium3 signatures + Kyber768 key exchange
- **ASIC-Resistant Mining**: RandomX Proof-of-Work
- **Fast Transactions**: 2-minute block time
- **Lightning Network**: Instant micropayments
- **Privacy**: Tor/I2P support
